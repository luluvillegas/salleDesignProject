Photography

I originally built this website to showcase my photography . I wanted a very minimal experience where the user could solely focus on the content: photos and text. This whole website is designed and built towards that experience. I also wanted to showcase my photography into small photo stories rather than a classic one-by-one photo gallery. Putting imagery and text together in order to bring a narrative of moods and atmospheres.
